Rebuking GotQuestions Using Their Own Words

1. Cultural Relativism Contradiction

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"Paul affirms the rightness of following that cultural mandate—to dispense with the head coverings on women would send the entirely wrong signal to the culture at large."

Response to the statement taken from: What is Cultural Relativism?

"Cultural relativism sees nothing inherently wrong (and nothing inherently good) with any cultural expression. So, the ancient Mayan practices of self-mutilation and human sacrifice are neither good nor bad; they are simply cultural distinctives."

2. Worldliness vs. Submission

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"In today’s culture, we no longer view a woman’s wearing of a head covering as a sign of submission."

Response to the statement taken from:

  • What does it mean that Christians are not of this world?
    "Christians, as part of Jesus' kingdom, are not of this world."
  • What does it mean to not conform to the pattern of this world?
    "As followers of Christ, we are not to be conformed to this world's pattern."

3. Authority of Scripture Undermined

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"God is far more concerned with an attitude of submission than an outward display of submission via a head covering."

Response to the statement taken from: What does it mean to dress modestly?

"Our clothes should demonstrate a right understanding of God, a right understanding of ourselves, and respect for the people we are around."

4. Symbolism and Modesty

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"The veil or covering on the head of a believing Corinthian wife showed that she was under the authority of her husband, and therefore under submission to God."

Response to the statement taken from: What Does it Mean to Dress Modestly?

"At the core of dressing modestly is reverence for God. Our outward appearance does communicate things to the watching world."

5. Biblical Influence

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"Scarves and hats are fashion accessories. A woman has the choice to wear a head covering if she views it as a sign of her submission."

Response to the statement taken from: How Much Influence is the Bible Supposed to Have on Society?

"When society doesn’t follow the principles that God gives us in the Bible, life simply doesn’t work as well."

6. Addressing solely the Corinthians? FALSE.

Taken from: Should Christian women wear head coverings?

"The apostle Paul is addressing an issue related to the Corinthian culture that was being allowed to disrupt the church."

Response to the statement taken from: 1 Corinthians 1:2

"To the church of God that is in Corinth, to those sanctified in Christ Jesus, called to be saints together with all those in every place call upon the name of our Lord Jesus Christ."
